About Hemant Fertilizers

A Symbol of Quality Since 1995

Our Mission

Established in 1995, Hemant Fertilizers Pvt. Ltd. has been a cornerstone in the agricultural community. We are dedicated to serving farmers with innovative, high-quality organic crop protection and plant supplements. Our objective is to bring the best technology to the farming community, upholding the highest standards of quality through dedicated field research, development, and manufacturing.

Our Leadership

Under the visionary leadership of our Chairman, Shri Rameshwar Kushwaha, and the strategic direction of our Marketing Director, Shri Surendra Kushwaha, HFPL has grown into a leading name in the agrochemical industry, committed to the prosperity of farmers.

The Power of Silicon

Transforming Crop Health and Resilience

Increased Resistance

Strengthens cell walls, making plants more resistant to pests, diseases, strong winds, and rain.

Enhanced Growth

Improves photosynthesis, nutrient uptake (K, P, Ca), and overall plant biomass, leading to higher yields.

Stress Tolerance

Alleviates abiotic stresses like drought, salt, and toxicity from minerals like Aluminum and Manganese.

Improved Soil Health

Enhances soil structure, water retention, and creates a favorable environment for beneficial microorganisms.

Better Yield & Quality

Increases shelf life, shine, and overall quality of fruits and vegetables, leading to better market value.

Sugarcane Specialist

Proven to significantly increase cane and sugar yields by addressing silicon deficiency in this high-demand crop.
